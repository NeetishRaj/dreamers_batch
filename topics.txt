Q. what's the difference between the two lines if any?

    console.log("hi");
    window.console.log("hi");

A. No, there is no difference.



Q. Guess the output.

    var a = 10;
    console.log(++window.a);

A. 11



Q. Guess the output.

    function some_function() {
        var a = 20;
        console.log(window.a);
    }

    some_function();

A. undefined
